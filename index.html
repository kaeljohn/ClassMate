What is ClassMate?

ClassMate is a comprehensive Student Performance & Attendance Information System designed to revolutionize how instructors manage their classrooms. This powerful platform allows educators to seamlessly manage student information, subject enrollment, attendance records, and performance scores all in one centralized location.

Our Purpose

The purpose of ClassMate is to help students and instructors master relational database design by integrating multiple academic-related datasets into a practical, real-world application. It trains users to effectively manage many-to-many relationships (such as students enrolled in multiple subjects), handle time-based data like attendance dates, and compute performance analytics using powerful SQL queries.

Powerful Analytics

ClassMate provides centralized tracking of attendance, quizzes, exams, and overall class standing. The system generates comprehensive analytic reports that summarize each student's attendance rate, performance trends, and subject rankings, giving instructors the insights they need to help every student succeed.
